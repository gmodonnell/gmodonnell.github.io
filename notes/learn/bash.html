The Bourne Again Shell

Bash is named after the Bourne Shell which is in turn named after Stephen Bourne who was absolutely cracked. As far as I know, the CLI is the closest you can get to the kernel, so it's important to know your way around this CLI and others. Typically, you can access a terminal with the Ctrl+Alt+T shortcut.
