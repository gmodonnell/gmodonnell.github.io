Cracking Passwords

Also known as password cracking, cracking passwords is a fun way to violate the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act.

Hashcat

Hashcat or John? The choice is up to you. I prefer hashcat because of their fun mascot. 


hashcat -m [hashcode] -a 3 -i [hashfile]

hashcat -m [hashcode] -a 0 [hashfile] [dictfile]

If the device you are on does not automatically utilize your GPU, specify it with -d 2 to make sure Hashcat is checking for the GPU. You will almost always want to crack on GPU over CPU.
