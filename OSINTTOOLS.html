These are some of my favorite tools for OSINT. Grouped by function, these are websites which I have found to be particularly useful. Be warned, most of these are clearnet and some require accounts.

Emails/Credentials

  • Hunter.io is good for finding corporate email accounts which are associated with a particular domain you may be interested in. Account required.
  • Have I Been Pwned, and Breach-Parse have both been good ways to see if any targets have already been compromised. Even if you have an old password, it could still be in use on another service the target has employed.
